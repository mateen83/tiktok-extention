Data Management

Permissions Explained

activeTab
Accesses the current tab only when you click the extension icon. No background tab access.
downloads
Required to save video files to your computer via Chrome's download manager.
storage
Stores your settings and download history locally. Nothing is sent externally.
tiktok.com host access
Runs the content script on TikTok pages to detect available videos. No data is collected.
